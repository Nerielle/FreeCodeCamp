Koh Samui

What to do on Samui

  • Rent a bike. It wil be very useful. You will see why.
  • Even if your hotel is on the nice beach, you should to visit many others. There are so many beaches and they are all different. And I suppose that any beach can vary depending on season. My favorite sand beach is Mae Nam. Because it is so calm.
  • Visit Walking Street. This is a place where you discover what is an Asian street food.
  • Enjoy seafood. It's delicious here. There is a big seafood "Crab" restaraunt not far from the Central Festival mall.
  • Take different types of massage. But it's depends of master. You'll be lucky if you find the good one.
  • Visit Buddhist Temples. One of the best is Wat Plai Laem. This temple is surrounded by a lake, which is teeming with fish.
  • Go to the snorkelling on Koh Tao.
  • Go to the Secret Buddha Garden. There is winding mountain road. Along the way you can visit the waterfalls.
